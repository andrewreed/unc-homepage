My Open Source Projects

The following programs are currently under development. I will upload them to GitHub once I have completed my research. Since my degree is being funded by the US Government, there will be no copyright associated with these programs.

dashem
A lightweight DASH emulator that can replicate a Netflix-like architecture and generate adaptive traffic from any number of distributed clients that are streaming "real movies". This emulator will be suitable for use in storage-limited environments such as GENI.

dashmon
A program that monitors a network interface (to include a DAG card acting as a passive tap) for live DASH streams. DASH streams are reported as they are identified. This programs relies on adudump for input.

DASH Detective
A Hadoop program that analyzes adudump logs and reports statistics relevant to our DASH Traffic Model.
