About Me

I am a Major in the U.S. Army with a B.S. in Computer Science from the United States Military Academy. I am currently attending the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill to obtain a Master's in Computer Science. Afterwards, I will return to West Point as an instructor in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department.

My research interests center on traffic analysis, specifically the effect on network performance as users eschew traditional media sources in favor of Internet-based streaming media. I am particularly interested in using "Big Data" tools (e.g. Hadoop) to enable large scale analysis of network traces. I am also interested in the effects on user privacy as media consumption shifts to the Internet.
